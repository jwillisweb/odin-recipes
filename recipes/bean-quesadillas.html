Refried Bean & Corn Quesadillas


Here's a step by step guide on how to make the perfect Refried Bean & Corn Quesadillas

What is it?

These mouthwatering quesadillas are packed with homemade refried beans, sweetcorn, spring onion and plenty of cheddar. Dip 'em in irresistibly smoky chipotle mayo, and serve with a salad to the side. Napkins at the ready!

Takes 35 minutes.

What do I need?

  • Red kidney beans (390g)
  • Canned sweetcorn (150g)
  • Ground cumin (1tsp)
  • Cheddar cheese (80g)
  • Ground smoked paprika (1tsp)
  • Spring onion
  • Chipotle paste (20g)
  • Plain tortillas (6pcs)
  • Garlic clove x2
  • Mayonnaise (30ml)
  • Lime
  • Baby leaf salad (50g)

You'll also need

olive oil, pepper, salt, water (or dietary alternatives)


How do I make it?

  1. Preheat the oven to 200°C/ 180°C (fan)/ gas 6
  2. Peel and finely chop (or grate) your garlic
  3. Drain and rinse your kidney beans
  4. Heat a pot with a drizzle of olive oil over a medium heat
  5. Once hot, add the chopped garlic and cook for 1 min
  6. Add your ground smoked paprika and ground cumin and cook for 30 secs further
  7. Add the drained kidney beans with 100ml cold water and bring to the boil
  8. Cook for 4-5 min or until the beans are beginning to soften
  9. Meanwhile, trim and finely slice your spring onion and add to a large mixing bowl
  10. Drain your sweetcorn and add it to the bowl
  11. Grate your cheddar cheese into the bowl, season with a generous pinch of salt and pepper and mix thoroughly – this is your cheesy sweetcorn mixture
  12. Once the kidney beans are beginning to break down, season with a generous pinch of salt and squeeze in the juice of half your lime
  13. Remove the beans from the heat and mash into a semi-smooth paste – these are your refried beans
  14. Divide the refried beans and cheesy sweetcorn mixture between your tortillas, covering only half
  15. Fold each tortilla over and press down firmly so you're left with 3 half moon-shaped sandwiches per person – these are your quesadillas
  16. Add the quesadillas to a baking tray (or two!)
  17. Place a second baking tray[s] on top to weigh them down slightly
  18. Put the stacked trays in the oven for 10 min or until the quesadillas are slightly browned and the cheese has melted – these are your refried bean & corn quesadillas
  19. Meanwhile, combine the juice of the remaining lime with 2 tbsp olive oil and a pinch of salt and pepper – this is your salad dressing
  20. Combine your chipotle paste (can't handle the heat? Go easy!) and mayo with a drizzle of olive oil – this is your chipotle mayo
  21. Slice the refried bean & corn quesadillas in half
  22. Wash your salad, then pat it dry with kitchen paper and toss it in the salad dressing
  23. Serve the halved refried bean & corn quesadillas alongside the dressed salad and the chipotle mayo for dipping
  24. Enjoy!
